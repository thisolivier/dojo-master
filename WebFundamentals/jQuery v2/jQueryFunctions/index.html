Welcome to the jQuery Demo!

We just used the click and show functions, for when you clicked that button, the wrapper was shown:

$('#begin').click(function(){ $('.wrapper').show(); } )

KEY

Click on the colourful things to make them do things.

Fade Away

$('.green').click(function(){ $(this).fadeOut(); })

Slide Toggle

$('.yellow').click(function(){ $(this).slideToggle(); });

Sharp to Round

$('.square').click(function(){ $(this).addClass('round'); });

Reveal the hidden ones

$('.square').click(function(){ $(this).addClass('round'); });

Append with my Classes

Bonus here, you have to click the heading

$('#append').on('click', 'h2', function(){
var classList = $(this).closest('.card').attr('class');
$(this).closest('.card').append(classList); });
